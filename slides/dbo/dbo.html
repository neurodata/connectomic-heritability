Statistical problem#

  • Want an independence test!

  • \(H_0: F(Genome, Connectome) = F(Genome)F(Connectome)\) \(H_A: F(Genome, Connectome) \neq F(Genome)F(Connectome)\)

  • Test statistic: Distance correlation

Statistical problem#

  • Want an independence test!

  • \(H_0: F(Genome, Connectome|Covariates) = F(Genome|Covariates)F(Connectome|Covariates)\) \(H_A: F(Genome, Connectome|Covariates) \neq F(Genome|Covariates)F(Connectome|Covariates)\)

  • Test statistic: Conditional distance correlation
